## Typography

The current logo uses three visual type styles:

- Elegant high-contrast serif for "Collectively" and the CD monogram.
- Flowing handwritten script for "Delanie".
- Widely tracked clean sans serif for small supporting text such as "Designs by Delanie", "PEPd", and the tagline.

Exact font files are not stored in this project yet. Until the original font names/files are added, choose fonts that preserve those three roles rather than substituting casual or overly modern styles.

Suggested web-safe / web-font direction:

- Serif: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or similar.
- Script: a graceful signature-style script matching the logo.
- Sans serif: Montserrat, Avenir, or similar clean geometric sans.

## Usage Notes

- Keep the site soft, elevated, personal, and polished.
- Use white space generously.
- Lead with deep sage and dusty mauve; use pale blush and mist blue-gray as quiet support colors.
- Avoid loud colors, harsh black-heavy layouts, neon accents, and generic corporate styling.
- Preserve the butterfly / delicate line-art feeling when adding decorative moments.
